  Tropical Orange, Banana, Kiwi smoothie:

    = Ingredients =
  • 1 Orange = Peeled orange slices
  • 2 Banana's = Peeled banana slices
  • 3 Kiwi's = Peeled Kiwi slices
  • Add 1 cup of water, 1/4 cup of coconut juice.
  • Optional: Add a piece of ginger for a spicy flavor.
    = INSTRUCTIONS =
  1. * A Blender *
  2. If Frozen smoothie = Add ice
  3. Add the sliced fruits and ingredients to the blender.
  4. blend until smooth.



 Mango Smoothie Blast:

    = Ingredients =
  • 1 cup of chopped mango's
  • 1/2 cup of almond milk
  • 3 spoons of Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 spoon of Honey
  • Optional: Add a piece of ginger for a spicy flavor.
    = INSTRUCTIONS =
  1. * A Blender *
  2. If Frozen smoothie = Add ice
  3. Add the sliced fruits and ingredients to the blender.
  4. blend until smooth.
